Emanating quarterly from above a laundromat on Boulevard St. Laurent, Montreal, Quebec, and for now, Canada. It's a Bunny provides shelter for fine new english fiction, poetry, reviews and art in an otherwise cold and brutal climate.

s u b m i s s i o n s

We welcome submissions. Wordperfect or Word documents may be sent via e-mail if uuencoded, or mail your work in ASCII as part of the body of the mail message. For information about our vernissage section or to submit non-text works, drop us a note and we'll make easy arrangements.

Our address is bunny@iti.qc.ca

It's a Bunny is produced quarterly on an entirely voluntary basis. We cannot pay for submissions, but your work will receive international exposure and you will retain all copyrights.

FOOTNOTE 

In Indian poems, said C.S. Lewis,
ultimate sexiness is meant

when Shiva praises slim Shakti for walking
-- a joke to us -- like an elephant.

To Shakti, to Shiva, so serious
that delicate gait, a dancerly sway

you've seen at the zoo, or the circus parade,
or in some 3 a.m. reverie.

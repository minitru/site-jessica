Emanating almost quarterly from above a laundromat on Boulevard St. Laurent, Montreal, Quebec, and for now, Canada. It's a Bunny provides shelter for fine new english fiction, poetry, essays and art in an otherwise cold and brutal climate.

s u b m i s s i o n s

We welcome submissions. Send just about anything as an attachment and we'll deal with it. Guidelines for submission are to be inferred from the magazine itself; if you think your work would fit, send it along and it may find a loving home here in the Bunny.

Our address is bunny@iti.qc.ca

It's a Bunny is produced almost quarterly on an entirely voluntary basis. We cannot pay for submissions, because we don't have the money. However your work will receive international exposure, and you will retain all copyrights.
